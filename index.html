Valladolid Snooker Club - 1st Snooker Club In The City!

  • We are the 1st and only place to provide all billiard fans a Snooker Hall in the city of Valladolid.
  • Snooker is a sport for people of all ages to enjoy. It also helps to keep your body and brain active.

Better Motor & Coordination Skills

  • Children and adults who play pool tend to develop better and sharper teamwork, strategizing, hand-eye coordination, planning and a variety of other skills to get ahead in life.
  • Snooker is a game of focus, which helps with concentration skills in almost every aspect of your life.

Healthy Body, Healthy Mind

  • Snooker has been scientifically proven by the University of Copenhagen to slow down the aging process as it helps maintain the body active through active and passive action.
  • Constantly stretching in snooker poses does wonders for the muscles of the body that people don't often use, helping to acquire toned muscles and even to lose weight with all the calories burned in the process.
